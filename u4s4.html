The cost for them to have the old water heater removed, the area prepared for the new one and the new water heater installed will cost them $90 per hour.

  15. Phyllis has been saving money for a long time to take a vacation with her daughter. After many months, she has saved $1875. She wants to spend her money wisely to make it stretch throughout the entire vacation, so she plans to spend $150 per day.
    1. If they spend $1500, how long is their vacation?

      \(1875-150d=375\)

      \(1875-150d-1875=375-1875\)

      \(-150d=-1500\)

      \(\frac{-150D}{-150}=\frac{\displaystyle-1500}{-150}\)

      \(d=10\)

      Their vacation was 10 days long.

    2. If they have no money left when they return and they stick to their $150 per day budget, how many days is their vacation?

      \(1875-150d=0\)

      \(1875-150d-1875=0-1875\)

      \(-150d=-1875\)

      \(\frac{-150D}{-150}=\frac{\displaystyle-1875}{-150}\)

      \(d=12.5\)

      Their vacation was 12.5 days long.
