Chapter 3

Classical (or Theoretical) Probability:

\(P(E)=\frac{Number\;of\;outcomes\;in\;event\;E}{Total\;number\;of\;outcomes\;in\;the\;sample\;space}\)

Empirical (or Statistical) Probability:

\(P(E)=\frac{Frequency\;of\;event\;E}{Total\;frequency}=\frac fn\)

Probability of a Complement:

\(P(E')=1-P(E)\)

Probability of occurrence of both events A and B:

\(P(A\;and\;B)=P(A)\cdot P(\left.B\right|A)\)

\(P(A\;and\;B)=P(A)\cdot P(B)\;if\;A\;and\;B\;are\;independent\)

Probability of occurrence of either A or B:

\(P(A\;or\;B)=P(A)\;+\;P(B)\;-\;P(A\;and\;B)\)

\(P(A\;or\;B)=P(A)\;+\;P(B)\;if\;A\;and\;B\;are\;mutually\;exclusive\)


Chapter 4

Mean of a Discrete Random Variable:

\(\mu={\textstyle\sum_{}^{}}x\;P(x)\)

Variance and Standard Deviation of a Discrete Random Variable:

Variance: \(\sigma^2={\textstyle\sum_{}^{}}\left(x-\mu\right)^2\;P(x)\)

Standard Deviation: \(\sigma=\sqrt{\sigma^2}=\sqrt{{\textstyle\sum_{}^{}}\left(x-\mu\right)^2\;P(x)}\)

Expected Value:

\(E(x)=\mu={\textstyle\sum_{}^{}}x\;P(x)\)

Binomial Probability of x successes in n trials:

\(P(x)={}_nC_x\;p^xq^{n-x}=\frac{n!}{(n-x)!x!}p^xq^{n-x}\)

Population Parameters of a Binomial Distribution:

Mean: \(\mu=np\)
Variance: \(\sigma^2=npq\)
Standard Deviation: \(\sigma=\sqrt{npq}\)
